Why it's important to groom your pet

Grooming helps rid the coat of shed hair and dead skin while spreading natural oils. Grooming your pet is a great way to spend quality time with them and build a stronger bond. It allows you to show your pet affection and care, which can help to strengthen your relationship. It can also help you to identify any health issues that your pet may have as well as improving your pet's appearance.

Is it okay to groom kittens and puppies?

Grooming puppies and kittens is acceptable. In fact, it's crucial to begin grooming your pet early on so that they get used to the activity and come to like it. Brushing or combing their fur, cutting their nails, cleaning their ears, and bathing them are all examples of grooming.
